## Troubleshooting

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Verification Email Not Received">
    If you don't receive the verification email:
    
    - Check your spam or junk folder
    - Ensure you entered the correct email address
    - Wait up to 15 minutes for delayed delivery
    - Click "Resend Verification" in your account settings
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Email Already in Use">
    If the system indicates your new email is already in use:
    
    - Confirm you don't have another account with that email
    - Try using an email alias if your provider supports it
    - Contact support if you believe there's an error
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Verification Link Expired">
    If your verification link has expired:
    
    1. Return to your account settings
    2. Click "Resend Verification"
    3. Complete the verification process within 24 hours
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
