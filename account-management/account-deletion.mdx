This guide explains how to delete your account and what happens to your data afterward.

<Warning>
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems.
</Warning>

## Important Information About Account Deletion

<Info>
At this time, the only way to delete your account is to contact our support team. Please reach out to support@greenzero.eu to initiate the account deletion process.
</Info>

## What Happens When You Delete Your Account

<Accordion title="What will be deleted">
- All your personal profile information
- Your activity history and preferences
- Any comments you've made across the platform
- Your personal documents and settings
- All personal information associated with your account
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What will be retained">
- Organization data and structure (account deletion does not delete the organization)
- Anonymized usage data for analytics purposes
- Any content you've shared publicly that others have referenced
- Records required for legal and compliance purposes
</Accordion>

## Data Removal Timeline

After your deletion request is processed:

1. **Immediate**: Your account becomes inaccessible
2. **Within 30 days**: Your personal data is removed from our active systems
3. **Within 90 days**: Your data is removed from our backups

<Note>
Some information may be retained for legal, security, or business purposes as outlined in our Privacy Policy.
</Note>

## Need Help?

If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance:

<Tip>
Contact our support team at support@greenzero.eu or use the live chat feature in your account settings.
</Tip>
